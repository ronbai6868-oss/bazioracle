The 12 Earthly Branches:
Animals, Elements & Hidden Stems 十二地支：
生肖、五行与藏干详解

8 min · BaZi Oracle · June 2026

Home首页 / Learn学习 / The 12 Earthly Branches十二地支

Every pillar in your BaZi chart has two parts: a Heavenly Stem on top and an Earthly Branch (地支) below. While stems are visible and direct, branches carry hidden depths — each one contains concealed elements called Hidden Stems (藏干) that quietly shape your destiny. 八字命盘的每一柱都有两部分：上面的天干和下面的地支。天干直白外露，地支则蕴含深意——每个地支都藏有称为藏干的隐秘五行，在暗中悄然影响你的命运。

What Are the Earthly Branches?什么是地支？

The 12 Earthly Branches are a cyclical system used in the Chinese calendar to mark years, months, days, and hours. You know them by their popular names — the 12 animals of the Chinese zodiac. But in BaZi, each branch carries far more meaning than just an animal symbol. 十二地支是中国历法中用于标记年、月、日、时的循环系统。你或许熟悉它们的通俗名称——十二生肖。但在八字命理中，每个地支的含义远不止一个动物符号那么简单。

Each branch belongs to a primary element, carries a season, and contains one to three hidden stems inside it. These hidden stems represent additional elemental energies that emerge under certain conditions and can profoundly affect how your chart operates. 每个地支都属于一种主要五行，对应一个季节，并内藏一到三个天干。这些藏干代表在特定条件下会显现的额外五行能量，能深刻影响你命盘的运作方式。

The Earthly Branches are the roots of your chart. Just as a tree's roots determine its strength and reach, your branches determine how deeply your elemental energies are anchored and how they interact with the world. 地支是你命盘的根基。正如树木的根决定其力量与延伸，地支决定了你的五行能量有多深厚的根基，以及它们如何与外部世界互动。

Why Hidden Stems Matter为什么藏干如此重要

Consider someone born in the Month of Yin (寅, the Tiger). The surface element is Yang Wood — but inside the Tiger lurk Yang Fire (丙) and Yang Earth (戊) as well. This means even without Fire or Earth appearing as stems in their chart, those energies are quietly present and can be activated by certain years or luck cycles. 设想一个生于寅月（虎月）的人。表面的五行是阳木——但虎中还暗藏着丙火（阳火）和戊土（阳土）。这意味着即使命盘天干中没有火或土出现，这些能量也悄然存在，并可在特定流年或大运中被激活。

Hidden stems are especially important when identifying your useful god (用神) — the element your chart most needs for balance. Often the resource you need is buried in a branch rather than sitting openly as a stem. 藏干在确定用神时尤为关键——用神是命盘最需要的那个平衡元素。通常，你所需要的元素往往深藏于地支中，而非以明显的天干形式出现。

Branch Clashes: When Animals Conflict地支相冲：生肖相克

Certain pairs of branches directly oppose each other across the calendar, creating what are called clashes (冲). The six pairs are: Rat–Horse, Ox–Goat, Tiger–Monkey, Rabbit–Rooster, Dragon–Dog, Snake–Pig. 某些地支在历法上正好相对，形成所谓的相冲。六对相冲为：子午冲、丑未冲、寅申冲、卯酉冲、辰戌冲、巳亥冲。

When a clash appears in your chart — either between two pillars or introduced by a luck cycle or year — it brings disruption, change, and sometimes breakthrough. A Rat–Horse clash in the career pillars, for example, often signals abrupt job changes or power struggles at work. 当命盘中出现相冲——无论是两柱之间本就存在，还是由大运流年引入——它都会带来动荡、变化，有时也意味着突破。例如，事业宫出现子午冲，往往预示着工作上的突然变动或权力角力。

Practical Application: Reading Your Own Branches实际应用：读懂你的地支

When you receive your BaZi chart, look at the four branches (the bottom characters of each pillar). Ask yourself: 拿到八字命盘后，观察四个地支（每柱的下方字符），问自己：

  • Which element does each branch belong to? Does it strengthen or weaken my Day Master?每个地支属于哪个五行？它是强化还是削弱我的日主？
  • Are any seasonal groupings complete (e.g., 寅卯辰 all present), creating a dominant element?是否形成了完整的季节三合局（例如寅卯辰同时出现），产生主导性的五行？
  • Are any clash pairs present? If so, which pillars (year, month, day, hour) are clashing — this tells you which life area is under tension.是否存在相冲地支？如果有，哪些柱（年、月、日、时）在相冲——这揭示了哪个人生领域处于张力之中。
  • What hidden stems are buried in your branches? These often explain traits or resources that "shouldn't" be there according to the visible stems alone.你的地支中藏有哪些藏干？这往往能解释仅凭天干无法说明的某些特质或资源。
