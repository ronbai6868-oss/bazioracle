How to Read Your BaZi Chart:
A Step-by-Step Guide 如何读懂你的八字命盘：
分步详解指南

10 min · BaZi Oracle · June 2026

Home首页 / Learn学习 / How to Read Your BaZi Chart如何读懂八字命盘

You have your Four Pillars chart in front of you — eight Chinese characters arranged in four columns. But what do they mean? This step-by-step guide walks you through the five essential layers of reading a BaZi chart, from the most basic to the most revealing. 你面前有一张四柱命盘——八个汉字排列成四列。但它们意味着什么？本指南将带你逐步了解解读八字命盘的五个核心层次，从最基础到最深入。

Step 1: Identify Your Day Master第一步：找到你的日主

The first thing to look at is the top character of the Day Pillar (日柱) — the third column from the right in a traditional chart layout. This single character is your Day Master (日主), and it is the lens through which everything else is interpreted. 首先查看日柱的天干——在传统命盘排列中，从右往左数第三列的上方字符。这个字符就是你的日主，是解读一切的核心视角。

Your Day Master tells you your core elemental identity: Are you Wood (growth-oriented), Fire (expressive), Earth (stable), Metal (principled), or Water (adaptive)? And are you Yang (bold, direct) or Yin (subtle, strategic)? 日主告诉你的核心五行身份：你是木（以成长为导向）、火（善于表达）、土（稳定踏实）、金（原则性强）还是水（适应力强）？你是阳性（大胆直接）还是阴性（内敛策略）？

The Day Master is not your zodiac sign or your personality type. It is your elemental DNA — the fundamental energy you were born with and must work both with and on throughout your life. 日主不是你的星座或性格类型，它是你的五行DNA——你与生俱来的根本能量，贯穿一生，既要顺应，也要修炼。

Step 2: Count the Elements第二步：统计五行

Next, look at all eight characters and count how many belong to each element. Include both the visible stems and the primary element of each branch. This gives you a rough picture of your chart's elemental balance — or imbalance. 接下来，观察全部八个字符，统计每种五行出现了多少次。同时计入天干和地支的主要五行。这给你一个命盘五行平衡（或失衡）的初步图景。

Ask yourself: Which elements appear multiple times? Which are missing entirely? A chart dominated by one element tends toward extremes — great strengths but also predictable weaknesses. A chart with no Fire, for example, may struggle with passion, visibility, and social warmth. 问自己：哪些五行多次出现？哪些完全缺失？被某一五行主导的命盘倾向于极端——有突出的优势，但也有可预见的弱点。例如，完全没有火的命盘，可能在热情、曝光度和社交温度上有所欠缺。

Step 3: Assess Day Master Strength第三步：判断日主强弱

In BaZi, it matters a great deal whether your Day Master is strong (旺) or weak (弱). A strong Day Master has plenty of support from other elements in the chart. A weak one is outnumbered or controlled by opposing forces. 在八字命理中，日主的旺或弱至关重要。旺的日主在命盘中获得充足的同类或生扶；弱的日主则寡不敌众，受到克制势力的压制。

The key factors that strengthen a Day Master are: 强化日主的关键因素包括：

  • Born in season: A Wood Day Master born in spring (February–April) is in season and naturally stronger.生于旺季：木日主生于春季（二至四月）即当令，天然较旺。
  • Same-element support: Other Wood stems or branches in the chart add fuel to the Day Master.同类扶助：命盘中其他木质天干或地支为日主增力。
  • Resource element present: The element that generates your Day Master (Water generates Wood) acts as a parent, strengthening it.印星生扶：生助日主的五行（水生木）如同父母，加强日主力量。

Understanding whether your Day Master is strong or weak determines which elements are beneficial (用神) and which are detrimental (忌神) — this is the core of practical BaZi analysis. 判断日主旺弱，决定了哪些五行对你有利（用神），哪些有害（忌神）——这是实用八字分析的核心。

Step 4: Identify Your Useful God (用神)第四步：找出用神

The Useful God (用神) is the single most important element your chart needs for balance and flourishing. It is the element that, when present in a luck cycle or year, tends to bring good fortune, clarity, and progress. 用神是命盘最需要的那个五行元素，是平衡与兴旺的关键。当某个大运或流年带来用神时，往往会带来好运、清晰和进展。

For a weak Day Master, the useful god is typically the resource element (that generates DM) or the companion element (same element as DM). For a strong Day Master, the useful god is typically the output element (that DM generates) or the wealth element (that DM controls). 对于弱日主，用神通常是印星（生助日主的五行）或比劫（与日主同类的五行）。对于旺日主，用神通常是食伤（日主所生的五行）或财星（日主所克的五行）。

Each pillar corresponds to a different life area and time period. Reading them together gives a narrative arc of your entire life: 每一柱对应不同的人生领域和时间段。综合解读构成你人生的完整叙事：

A Simple Reading Framework简易解读框架

When you first receive your chart, follow this sequence: 拿到命盘时，按以下顺序解读：

  • 🔍 Find Day Master → know your core element🔍 找日主 → 了解核心五行
  • ⚖️ Count elements → spot what's abundant and what's missing⚖️ 统计五行 → 发现多余和缺失的五行
  • 💪 Assess DM strength → determine if you are strong or weak day master💪 判断日主强弱 → 确定你是旺日主还是弱日主
  • ⭐ Identify Useful God → know which element to attract and cultivate⭐ 找出用神 → 知道应该引入和培养哪个五行
  • 🗺️ Read pillars → map each column to a life area for personalised insights🗺️ 逐柱解读 → 将每一柱与人生领域对应，获取个性化洞见
