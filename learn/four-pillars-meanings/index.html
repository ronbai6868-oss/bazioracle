Year, Month, Day & Hour Pillars:
What Each Column Reveals 年柱、月柱、日柱、时柱：
每一柱揭示了什么

9 min · BaZi Oracle · June 2026

Home首页 / Learn学习 / Four Pillars Meanings四柱含义详解

Your BaZi chart has four columns — the Year Pillar, Month Pillar, Day Pillar, and Hour Pillar. Each column contains a Heavenly Stem and an Earthly Branch, and each tells a distinct story about a different dimension of your life. Understanding what each pillar governs turns a string of Chinese characters into a meaningful map of your destiny. 你的八字命盘有四列——年柱、月柱、日柱和时柱。每列各含一个天干和一个地支，分别讲述你人生不同维度的故事。了解每一柱所主宰的领域，能将一串汉字转化为有意义的命运地图。

The Year Pillar (年柱): Roots and Origins年柱：根基与来源

The Year Pillar is determined by the Chinese solar year of your birth. It is the outermost pillar — the one most visible to the world — and represents your connection to the larger forces that shaped you before you had any say in the matter. 年柱由你出生的农历年份决定，是最外层的一柱——最为世界所见——代表着在你尚无选择权之前那些塑造你的宏大力量。

The Year Pillar governs: 年柱主管：

  • Ancestry and family heritage — the elemental patterns passed down through your bloodline祖先与家族传承——通过血脉传承下来的五行模式
  • Early childhood (birth to age 15) — the environmental energies that surrounded your formative years童年时期（0至15岁）——围绕你成长初期的环境能量
  • Social reputation and public image — how you are perceived by society at large社会声誉与公众形象——社会大众如何看待你
  • Grandparents' influence — the generational patterns you inherited祖父母的影响——你所承继的代际模式

Think of the Year Pillar as the soil you were planted in. You didn't choose it, but its mineral composition profoundly affects what you grow into. 将年柱想象成你被种下的土壤。你没有选择它，但它的矿物成分深刻影响着你的成长方向。

The Month Pillar (月柱): Career and Ambition月柱：事业与抱负

The Month Pillar is calculated from the solar month of your birth — specifically the 节气 (solar terms), not the lunar months. It is considered the most powerful pillar for career and life achievement, and is sometimes called the "career pillar." 月柱根据你出生的节气月份计算——特指节气，而非阴历月份。它被认为是事业和人生成就方面最有力量的一柱，有时也被称为"事业宫"。

The Month Pillar governs: 月柱主管：

  • Career path and professional drive — your natural vocational tendencies and ambitions事业方向与职业驱动力——你天然的职业倾向和抱负
  • Young adulthood (ages 16–30) — the critical years of establishing yourself青年时期（16至30岁）——确立自我的关键年份
  • Parents' influence — particularly your relationship with authority figures父母的影响——尤其是与权威人物的关系
  • Siblings and peers — your competitive and collaborative instincts兄弟姐妹与同龄人——你的竞争与合作本能
  • Seasonal element strength — whether your Day Master is in season (旺) or out of season (弱)季节五行强度——你的日主是当令（旺）还是失令（弱）

Because the Month Pillar determines whether your Day Master is in its strongest season, it has an outsized influence on the entire chart's balance. A Wood Day Master born in a Wood month (February–March) is inherently stronger than the same Day Master born in a Metal month (August–September). 因为月柱决定了日主是否处于最旺盛的季节，所以它对整个命盘平衡有着举足轻重的影响。生于木月（二至三月）的木日主，天然比生于金月（八至九月）的同一日主更旺。

The Day Pillar (日柱): Self and Partnership日柱：自我与伴侣

The Day Pillar is the heart of the entire BaZi chart. The Day Stem (upper character) is your Day Master — your core elemental identity, the "I" through which all other influences are experienced. The Day Branch (lower character) is called the Spouse Palace (夫妻宫) and carries information about your romantic life and partnerships. 日柱是整个八字命盘的核心。日干（上方字符）是你的日主——你的核心五行身份，是体验一切其他影响的"自我"。日支（下方字符）称为夫妻宫，承载着你感情生活和伴侣关系的信息。

The Day Pillar governs: 日柱主管：

  • Core identity and personality — your fundamental elemental nature (Day Master)核心身份与性格——你根本的五行特质（日主）
  • Marriage and romantic partnerships — the energy of your ideal partner lives in the Day Branch婚姻与感情关系——理想伴侣的能量蕴藏在日支中
  • Prime adulthood (ages 31–50) — the years of peak responsibility and relationship formation成熟期（31至50岁）——责任最重、关系最深的年份
  • Inner world and private self — the "you" that close friends and partners experience内心世界与私下的自我——密友和伴侣所接触到的那个"你"

The element hidden in the Day Branch (Spouse Palace) often describes the type of partner that will bring you the most harmony. For example, if your Day Branch hides a strong Water element, a Water-type partner (深思熟虑、有智慧) may be especially compatible. 藏于日支（夫妻宫）中的五行，往往能描述最能给你带来和谐的伴侣类型。例如，若你的日支藏有强水，水型伴侣（深思熟虑、智慧丰富）可能特别相配。

The Hour Pillar (时柱): Legacy and Inner Life时柱：遗产与内心生命

The Hour Pillar is determined by your birth hour — each two-hour block corresponds to one of the 12 Earthly Branches. It is the most private pillar, the one furthest from public view, and it carries the energy of your deepest self. 时柱由你的出生时辰决定——每两小时对应十二地支中的一个。它是最私密的一柱，距离公众视野最远，承载着你最深层自我的能量。

The Hour Pillar governs: 时柱主管：

  • Children and the next generation — your relationship with children and your legacy子女与下一代——你与子女的关系以及你的传承
  • Later life (age 51 onwards) — the energies that will shape your retirement and legacy years晚年（51岁以后）——塑造你晚年和传承阶段的能量
  • Hidden desires and private dreams — what you truly want beneath social expectations隐秘愿望与私下梦想——社会期望之下你真正渴望的东西
  • Spiritual and philosophical tendencies — your inner life and relationship with meaning精神与哲学倾向——你的内心生活和对意义的追寻

The Hour Pillar is the part of you that never quite fits the roles others assign you. It is the quiet dream you carry, the life you secretly wonder about, and the legacy you most deeply wish to leave behind. 时柱是你身上那个从未完全适应他人所赋予角色的部分。它是你心中默默守护的梦想，是你暗自探索的那种生活，也是你内心深处最渴望留下的遗产。

Reading the Pillars as a Complete Story将四柱作为完整故事来解读

The four pillars are not isolated snapshots — they interact with each other. Clashes between pillars (e.g., a Rat in the Year Branch clashing with a Horse in the Month Branch) indicate tension between those life areas and time periods. Combinations and harmonies between pillars suggest natural flow and support. 四柱并非孤立的片段——它们相互影响。柱间相冲（例如年支的子与月支的午相冲）表示这两个人生领域和时期之间的张力；柱间相合则意味着自然的流动与扶助。

A skilled BaZi reading weaves the four pillars into a coherent narrative: how your origins (Year) shaped your ambitions (Month), which formed your identity (Day), and what legacy you are building (Hour). Together they trace the arc of an entire human life. 精熟的八字解读将四柱编织成连贯的叙事：你的来源（年）如何塑造了你的抱负（月），进而形成你的身份（日），以及你正在构建什么样的遗产（时）。四柱共同勾勒出整个人生的弧线。
