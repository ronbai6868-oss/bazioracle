What Is Your Day Master?
The Core of Your BaZi 什么是日主？
你的八字核心

5 min · BaZi Oracle · June 2026

Home首页 / Learn学习 / What Is Your Day Master?什么是日主？

In BaZi, your Four Pillars are made of four pairs of Chinese characters — one for Year, Month, Day, and Hour. The top character of the Day Pillar is called the Day Master (日主), and it is the single most important element in your entire chart. 在八字命理中，你的四柱由四对汉字组成——分别对应年、月、日、时。日柱的天干称为日主（日主），是整个命盘中最重要的一个元素。

Why the Day Master Matters为什么日主如此重要

Think of the Day Master as your core personality and elemental identity. Every other pillar in your chart is interpreted in relationship to it. If your Day Master is Yang Wood (甲), then Water in your chart is supportive, while Metal creates friction. 把日主想象成你的核心性格和五行身份。命盘中的其他所有柱，都是相对于日主来解读的。例如，如果你的日主是甲木（阳木），那么命盘中的水对你是滋养支持的，而金则会带来压力和阻力。

Knowing your Day Master allows you to immediately understand which elements help you and which create friction — in career, relationships, health and finances. 了解你的日主，让你立即明白哪些五行元素对你有益、哪些带来阻力——这适用于事业、感情、健康和财运各个方面。

The Day Master is not who you want to be — it is who you already are at your deepest level. Working with it rather than against it is the foundation of living in harmony with your chart. 日主不是你想成为的人——而是你在最深层面已然是的那个人。顺应日主而非逆其而为，是与命盘和谐共处的根基。

How to Find Your Day Master如何找到你的日主

The Day Master is calculated from your exact birth date using the traditional Chinese calendar. Even one day's difference can completely change your Day Master. 日主通过你的精确出生日期，按照中国传统历法计算得出。哪怕只差一天，日主也可能完全不同。
