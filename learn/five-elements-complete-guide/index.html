The Five Elements:
Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal & Water 五行详解：
木、火、土、金、水

8 min · BaZi Oracle · June 2026

Home首页 / Learn学习 / Five Elements Guide五行详解

The Five Elements (五行, Wǔ Xíng) are the foundation of Chinese metaphysics. In BaZi, they are not abstract concepts — they are forces that shape personality, relationships, health, wealth and life events. Understanding how they interact is the master key to reading any Four Pillars chart. 五行（木火土金水）是中国命理学的根基。在八字中，它们不是抽象概念，而是塑造性格、关系、健康、财运和人生事件的真实力量。理解五行如何相互作用，是解读任何四柱命盘的核心密钥。

🌿 Wood — 木 (Mù)🌿 木（Mù）

Season: Spring · Direction: East · Colour: Green季节：春 · 方位：东 · 颜色：绿色

Wood energy is the energy of growth, upward movement, and new beginnings. People with strong Wood in their chart tend to be idealistic, principled, and driven by a clear sense of purpose. 木之能量，是成长、向上运动与新生的力量。命盘中木旺之人，往往理想主义、原则性强，被清晰的使命感所驱动。

  • Strengths:优势： Creativity, compassion, ambition, persistence, ability to grow under pressure创造力、慈悲心、雄心壮志、锲而不舍、逆境成长的能力
  • Challenges:挑战： Rigidity, difficulty adapting, tendency to be over-idealistic固执、适应力欠佳、容易过于理想化
  • Career:适合领域： Education, writing, law, health, environmental fields教育、写作、法律、健康、环保领域
  • Balancing objects:补木物品： Green crystals, jade, plants, wooden decor, bamboo绿色水晶、翡翠、植物、木质摆件、竹制品

🔥 Fire — 火 (Huǒ)🔥 火（Huǒ）

Season: Summer · Direction: South · Colour: Red季节：夏 · 方位：南 · 颜色：红色

Fire energy is the energy of warmth, expansion, and connection. Fire Day Masters are typically charismatic, expressive and naturally social — they light up every room they enter. 火之能量，是温暖、扩张与连接的力量。火命日主之人，通常极具魅力、善于表达、天生爱社交——走到哪里都能点亮全场。

  • Strengths:优势： Passion, intuition, leadership, charisma, ability to inspire others热情、直觉、领导力、个人魅力、激励他人的能力
  • Challenges:挑战： Impulsiveness, inconsistency, emotional volatility, risk of burnout冲动、三分钟热度、情绪起伏、容易透支精力
  • Career:适合领域： Entertainment, media, marketing, politics, entrepreneurship娱乐、媒体、营销、政治、创业
  • Balancing objects:补火物品： Red crystals, carnelian, candles, red decor红色水晶、红玛瑙、红色水晶、蜡烛、红色装饰

⛰️ Earth — 土 (Tǔ)⛰️ 土（Tǔ）

Season: All transitions · Direction: Centre · Colour: Yellow/Brown季节：四季交接 · 方位：中央 · 颜色：黄色/棕色

Earth energy is the energy of stability, nourishment, and grounding. Earth people are the bedrock of any team — reliable, trustworthy, and creating safety for those around them. 土之能量，是稳定、滋养与接地的力量。土命之人是任何团队的定海神针——可靠、值得信赖，为身边的人创造安全感。

  • Strengths:优势： Reliability, patience, practical wisdom, loyalty, caretaking ability可靠、耐心、实际智慧、忠诚、照顾他人的天赋
  • Challenges:挑战： Stubbornness, over-caution, tendency to carry others' burdens固执、过于谨慎、容易扛下他人的重担
  • Career:适合领域： Real estate, finance, medicine, hospitality, management房产、金融、医疗、酒店服务、管理
  • Balancing objects:补土物品： Yellow crystals, tiger eye, terracotta, earthy tones, stones黄色水晶、虎眼石、陶器、土黄色系、各类矿石

⚔️ Metal — 金 (Jīn)⚔️ 金（Jīn）

Season: Autumn · Direction: West · Colour: White/Gold季节：秋 · 方位：西 · 颜色：白色/金色

Metal energy is the energy of precision, clarity, and refinement. Metal people have high standards and are natural at systems, structure, and cutting away what is unnecessary. 金之能量，是精准、清晰与精炼的力量。金命之人标准高、善于建立系统与结构，擅长去芜存菁。

  • Strengths:优势： Precision, discipline, integrity, analytical ability, excellence精准、自律、正直、分析力、追求卓越
  • Challenges:挑战： Perfectionism, inflexibility, emotional coolness, vulnerability issues完美主义、不够灵活、情感疏离、不善于展露弱点
  • Career:适合领域： Finance, law, medicine, engineering, quality control金融、法律、医疗、工程、品质管理
  • Balancing objects:补金物品： White crystals, white jade, clear quartz, silver jewellery, metallic decor白色水晶、白玉、白水晶、银饰、金属摆件

🌊 Water — 水 (Shuǐ)🌊 水（Shuǐ）

Season: Winter · Direction: North · Colour: Black/Deep Blue季节：冬 · 方位：北 · 颜色：黑色/深蓝

Water energy is the energy of depth, wisdom, and flow. Water people are natural thinkers and strategists — they see beneath the surface and understand patterns that others miss. 水之能量，是深度、智慧与流动的力量。水命之人天生善于思考和谋划——能看透表象，洞察他人忽略的规律。

  • Strengths:优势： Intelligence, adaptability, resourcefulness, strategic thinking, empathy智慧、适应力、随机应变、战略思维、深度同理心
  • Challenges:挑战： Over-thinking, tendency toward fear, difficulty with commitment过度思虑、容易焦虑恐惧、难以做出承诺
  • Career:适合领域： Research, psychology, writing, technology, consulting, arts研究、心理学、写作、科技、咨询、艺术
  • Balancing objects:补水物品： Black/dark blue crystals, lapis lazuli, black黑色/深蓝水晶、深色石材、青金石、黑碧玺

The Two Key Cycles两大核心循环

The Generating Cycle (相生) — Elements Supporting Each Other相生循环——五行相互滋养

Wood feeds Fire → Fire creates Earth → Earth holds Metal → Metal collects Water → Water nourishes Wood. This cycle represents nurturing, growth, and supportive relationships. 木生火 → 火生土 → 土生金 → 金生水 → 水生木。这个循环代表滋养、成长和相互支持的关系。

The Controlling Cycle (相克) — Elements Checking Each Other相克循环——五行相互制约

Wood breaks Earth → Earth absorbs Water → Water douses Fire → Fire melts Metal → Metal cuts Wood. This cycle represents authority, discipline, and necessary friction. 木克土 → 土克水 → 水克火 → 火克金 → 金克木。这个循环代表权威、约束和必要的摩擦。

In a well-balanced chart, the generating and controlling cycles work together to create dynamic harmony — like a well-functioning ecosystem where no single element dominates. 在均衡的命盘中，相生与相克共同运作，创造出动态的和谐——就像一个运转良好的生态系统，没有任何单一元素占绝对主导。
