Heavenly Stems & Earthly Branches:
The Building Blocks of BaZi 天干与地支：
八字命理的基石

7 min · BaZi Oracle · June 2026

Home首页 / Learn学习 / Heavenly Stems & Earthly Branches天干与地支

Before you can read a BaZi chart, you need to understand its alphabet. BaZi uses a system of 10 Heavenly Stems (天干) and 12 Earthly Branches (地支) — a total of 22 characters that combine to form every possible date, time, and destiny pattern in the Chinese calendar. 在读懂八字命盘之前，你需要了解它的"字母表"。八字使用十天干和十二地支——共22个字符，它们组合在一起，构成了中国历法中一切可能的日期、时间和命运格局。

The 10 Heavenly Stems (天干)十天干

The Heavenly Stems represent the visible, active forces in your chart. They sit at the top of each pillar and express elemental energy in its purest, most direct form. There are 10 stems because each of the Five Elements appears in both Yang (positive, active) and Yin (receptive, gentle) forms. 天干代表命盘中显性、主动的力量。它们位于每柱的上方，以最纯粹、最直接的形式表达五行能量。天干共十个，因为五行各有阴阳两种形态：阳（积极主动）和阴（接纳柔和）。

Yang vs. Yin: What's the Difference?阳与阴：有何不同？

Both 甲 (Yang Wood) and 乙 (Yin Wood) belong to the Wood element — but they express it very differently. Yang Wood (甲) is like a tall, proud oak: firm, upright, ambitious, slow to bend. Yin Wood (乙) is like a vine: flexible, adaptive, quietly persistent, able to grow around obstacles rather than through them. 甲（阳木）和乙（阴木）同属木行——但表达方式截然不同。阳木（甲）如参天大树：挺拔、自尊、志向远大、不易弯折。阴木（乙）如藤蔓：灵活、适应性强、静静持守，能绕过障碍而非硬穿而过。

This same principle applies across all five elements. Yang versions are more visible, forceful, and externally directed. Yin versions are subtler, more internal, and often more strategically effective. 这一原理适用于所有五行。阳性更显眼、更强劲、更向外发散；阴性则更内敛、更内化，往往在策略上更为有效。

The 60-Year Cycle: Stems and Branches Together六十甲子：天干与地支的组合

On their own, the 10 stems cycle endlessly. The 12 branches cycle endlessly. But when they are paired together, it takes exactly 60 years before the same combination repeats — this is the famous 60-Year (Sexagenary) Cycle, also known as 六十甲子 (Liùshí Jiǎzǐ). 单独来看，十天干循环不止，十二地支也循环不止。但当它们配对组合时，恰好需要60年才会出现同一组合——这就是著名的六十甲子循环。

This 60-year cycle underlies the entire Chinese calendar. Your birth year, month, day, and hour are each expressed as one of these 60 possible combinations. The Year Pillar of 2026, for example, is 丙午 (Yang Fire Horse). 这个六十年周期是整个中国历法的基础。你的出生年、月、日、时各自对应六十甲子中的某一组合。例如，2026年的年柱是丙午（阳火午马）。

The 60-year cycle explains why a 60th birthday is so significant in Chinese culture — it marks the completion of a full destiny cycle, a return to one's original elemental beginning. 六十年循环解释了为何在中国文化中60岁生日（甲子寿）如此重要——它标志着一个完整命运周期的终结，也是对原初五行起点的回归。

How Stems and Branches Form Your Four Pillars天干地支如何构成你的四柱

Your BaZi chart, or Four Pillars, is built from four of these 60-combination pairs: 你的八字命盘（四柱）由其中四个六十甲子组合构成：

  • Year Pillar (年柱)年柱 — Determined by the Chinese solar year of your birth. Reflects your generational energy, ancestry, and early childhood.由你出生的农历年份决定。反映你的时代能量、祖先影响和童年经历。
  • Month Pillar (月柱)月柱 — Determined by the solar month (节气, solar term). Reflects your career drive, ambitions, and the energies of ages 16–30.由节气月份决定。反映你的事业驱动力、抱负以及16至30岁阶段的能量。
  • Day Pillar (日柱)日柱 — Determined by your exact birth date. The Day Stem is your Day Master — your core identity. Reflects partnerships and your 30s–40s.由你的精确出生日期决定。日柱天干即日主——你的核心身份。反映伴侣关系和30至40岁阶段的能量。
  • Hour Pillar (时柱)时柱 — Determined by your birth hour (each 2-hour block). Reflects your inner world, children, and later life after 50.由出生时辰（每两小时一个时辰）决定。反映你的内心世界、子女缘分和50岁后的晚年。

The Stem–Branch Relationship: Heaven and Earth天干地支的关系：天与地

In Chinese cosmology, Heavenly Stems represent the forces of Heaven — visible, expressive energy that moves outward. Earthly Branches represent Earth — grounded, hidden, containing the seeds of what will emerge. 在中国宇宙观中，天干代表天之力——显性的、向外表达的能量；地支代表地之力——扎根的、隐藏的，蕴含着将要萌发的种子。

Together they create a complete picture. Your stems show what the world sees — your visible personality and external circumstances. Your branches show what is underneath — your deeper nature, hidden resources, and the tensions you carry. 两者合在一起，构成完整的图景。天干展示世界所见——你的外在性格和环境；地支揭示底层——你更深层的本质、潜在资源以及内在的张力。
