3. Cookies and Local Storage3. Cookie 和本地存储

Our website uses browser localStorage to cache your BaZi reading results locally on your device for convenience (so you can return to your results without re-entering data). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data. 我们的网站使用浏览器本地存储（localStorage）在您的设备上本地缓存您的八字解读结果，方便您无需重新输入数据即可返回查看。这些数据不会离开您的设备，也不会传输到我们的服务器。您可以随时通过清除浏览器站点数据来删除它。
