Refund Policy 退款政策

Last updated: July 2026 最后更新：2026年7月

✦ Our commitment: We want you to be completely satisfied. If something goes wrong, we will make it right. ✦ 我们的承诺：我们希望您完全满意。如有任何问题，我们将妥善解决。

1. Digital Products — General Policy1. 数字产品——总体政策

All purchases on BaZi Oracle are for digital products (AI-generated readings and downloadable wallpaper images) that are delivered immediately upon payment. Because the content is delivered instantly and consumed upon access, we generally do not offer refunds once a reading has been generated and viewed. 八字神算上的所有购买均为数字产品（AI生成的解读及可下载的壁纸图片），付款后立即交付。由于内容即时交付且在访问时即被使用，解读内容一经生成并查看，我们通常不提供退款。

2. Eligible Refund Situations2. 符合退款条件的情况

We will issue a full refund in the following situations: 在以下情况下，我们将全额退款：

  • Technical failure: You completed payment but the reading or download was never delivered due to a technical error on our side. 技术故障：您完成了付款，但由于我们方面的技术错误，解读或下载从未交付。
  • Duplicate charge: You were charged more than once for the same product due to a payment processing error. 重复收费：由于支付处理错误，您为同一产品被收费超过一次。
  • Corrupted or unreadable content: The delivered file is corrupted, blank, or otherwise unusable. 内容损坏或无法读取：交付的文件损坏、空白或无法使用。
  • Unauthorised transaction: You did not authorise the payment and can demonstrate this to us. 未经授权的交易：您未授权该笔付款，并能向我们证明这一点。

3. How to Request a Refund3. 如何申请退款

To request a refund, email us at support@getbazioracle.com within 7 days of your purchase with: 如需申请退款，请在购买后 7天内 发送邮件至 support@getbazioracle.com，并附上：

  • Your order ID or transaction reference您的订单编号或交易参考号
  • The email address used for purchase购买时使用的电子邮件地址
  • A brief description of the issue问题的简要说明

We will respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed by Paddle and typically appear in your account within 5–10 business days. 我们将在2个工作日内回复。获批的退款由 Paddle 处理，通常在5–10个工作日内到账。

4. Non-Refundable Situations4. 不符合退款条件的情况

We are unable to offer refunds in the following cases: 以下情况不予退款：

  • You have already accessed and read your full reading您已访问并阅读了完整的解读内容
  • You disagree with the interpretation or predictions in your reading您不认同解读中的内容或预测
  • You entered incorrect birth data and received a reading for the wrong chart您输入了错误的出生数据并收到了错误命盘的解读
  • More than 7 days have passed since purchase购买后已超过7天

Note: If you entered incorrect birth data, we encourage you to re-enter the correct date — a free chart is available to everyone, and you can purchase a new reading for the correct birth data. 注意：如果您输入了错误的出生数据，我们建议您重新输入正确日期——免费命盘向所有人开放，您可以为正确的出生数据购买新的解读。
